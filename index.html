ABOUT

The Woman Behind the Lens

Meet Tiffany, the creative force known as Kentucky Red, who's redefining vintage photography in the digital age

Hello from Adelaide, Australia! I'm Tiffany, also known as Kentucky Red, a vintage-inspired model, photographer, and content creator who brings a unique blend of classic elegance and modern creativity to every project.

With a passion for storytelling through visual art, I specialize in creating compelling content that captures the essence of brands while maintaining an authentic, timeless aesthetic.

My work spans from intimate portrait sessions to dynamic brand campaigns, always with an eye for detail and a commitment to excellence. Whether you're looking for creative styling, brand campaigns, or Instagram promotions, I bring a professional yet personal approach to every collaboration.
